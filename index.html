Howdy! I am a senior research scientist at Meta (Meta Superintelligence Labs (MSL)), working on LLM post-training for Meta AI Assistant.

Previously, I was a member of the infolab led by Prof. James Caverlee in the Computer Science & Engineering Department at Texas A&M University. Before that, I was at the ICA lab in the School of Computer Science and Software Engineering at East China Normal University and worked with Dr. Qinmin Hu and Dr. Liang He. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Donghua University.
